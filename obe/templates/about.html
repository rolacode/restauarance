Based in Nigeria, Lagos, Obelawo Restaurant is a family owned Mediterranean restaurant, focused on traditional recipes served with a modern twist.

The chefs draw inspiration from Italian, Greek, and Turkish culture and have a menu of 12-15 items that they rotate seasonally.

The restaurant has a rustic and relaxed atmosphere with moderate prices, making it a popular place for a meal any time of the day.

Obelawo is owned by a Nigeria man, Rotimi Olanrewaju, who came back from the United States to pursue their shared dream of owning a restaurant.

To craft the menu, Rotimi relies on family recipes and his experience as a chef in Nigeria.

Deborah one of his daughter does all the marketing for the restaurant and led the effort to expand the menu beyond classic Italian to incorporate additional cuisines from the mediterranean region.
